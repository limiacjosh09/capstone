ABOUT PAGE

This web app does not require you to login. There are no right or wrong answers, the system follows a Family Feud-style concept, where answers are ranked based on how often they are chosen. There are no right or wrong answers it all depends on how people perceive and respond to the questions. The top answers change dynamically depending on how many times each response has been selected.

Important Reminder: You can only answer once. If you reopen an answered scenario, you will only be able to view the top answers, and your response will not be counted.
